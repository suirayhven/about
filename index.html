Brief

Sui’ra is a feisty and captivating catboy with a penchant for seizing opportunities and achieving his desires, be it uncovering secrets, gaining favors, or forging bonds. His lively nature and inate charisma make him an irresistible presence in any situation.

During the day, he aids the Warrior of Darkness in their quest to save the world. Come nightfall, he mingles with all kinds of people in the Eorzean underground, unveiling secrets for the Scions and himself.

Character

Much of Sui’ra’s background remains shrouded in mystery, with most of it either classified or unknown, even to himself. Some speculate that even he himself possesses little recollection of his own past, rendering him a blank slate of sorts. This enigmatic quality uniquely qualifies him for his role as an undercover agent, as he harbors nothing worth losing. Years of improvised lying and storytelling have made him remarkably skilled in the art of deception, and he manages to elude even the keenest of minds and eyes.

Despite his professional excellence, Sui’ra’s personal life is plagued by shortcomings of another sort. His shrouded nature and deceptive tendencies have hindered his ability to form meaningful or intimate bonds. The absence of such connections leaves him vulnerable, as the longing for genuine companionship weighs heavily on him. When confronted with reminders of this unfulfilled aspect of his life, the psychological toll becomes overwhelming, eventually causing him to crack under pressure.

Appearance

Sui’ra stands at a diminutive height of 5’1”, a stark contrast to the majority of his Miqo’te brethren. His ash-blond hair is loosely styled into a messy left-parted do, with prominent bangs covering a portion of his visage. Despite his initially slim appearance, Sui’ra is fairly toned and can stand his ground in many a physical challenge.

His profession requires him to stay presentable and Sui’ra laid to rest his usual, more traditional clothing style in favor of a more modern one. His style is inspired in part by the bygone fashions of Garlemald as well as the, to Eorzean eyes, avant-garde attire of Solution Nine.
